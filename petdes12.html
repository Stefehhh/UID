Green Parrot Bird Toy BASKET STACKER

Product Description

Green Parrot Toy BASKET STACKER is a natural bird toy featuring reeds, rattan and coconut fibre. A string of rope holds both baskets together. Your bird will have plenty of fun shredding, chewing and playing with this toy. Basket Stacker contains a wooden link to hang inside your bird's cage.
Swap toys on a weekly basis to keep your birds mind active and engaged with life.
Always supervise bird while playing with toys.
Use correct size toy for your bird.
Remove toy if damaged.
Suitable for:
Small Birds such as Budgies, Canaries, Finches & other similar sized birds.
Small Parrots such as Budgies, Cockatiels, Green Cheek Conure, Scaly Lorikeet & other similar sized birds.
Medium Parrots such as Indian Ringnecks, Alexandrine, Sun Conure, Rainbow Lorikeet & other similar sized birds.
Large Parrots such as Eclectus, African Grey, Amazon, Major Mitchel Cockatoo, Corella & other similar sized birds
Dimensions:
Green Parrot Toy BASKET STACKER measures approx. 14cm x 32cm (when hanging).
