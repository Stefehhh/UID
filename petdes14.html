Finch Nest Hooded (Rattan) Small

Product Description

These nests come with 2 metal hooks so they can be hung on the side of an avairy or bird cage. Finches require nesting material like feathers and swamp grass which are to be left on the floor of the enclosure. The finches will then transport the material into the rattan nest to create a safe and comfortable inner lining for the nest.
Comes in two sizes:
Small - 8.8 x 10.7 cm (approx)
Large - 14 x 16 cm (approx)
